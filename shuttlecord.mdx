## Getting Started with ShuttleCord

ShuttleCord is a comprehensive Discord server management tool equipped with various features such as Joiner, Leaver, Spammer, and more. This guide will walk you through the process of acquiring a license and utilizing the tool's capabilities.

### Purchase a License

To begin using ShuttleCord, you need to acquire a license. Follow these steps to purchase your license:

1. Visit `[discord.gg/shuttle-services](https://discord.gg/shuttle-services)`.
2. Explore the available licensing options and choose the one that best suits your needs.
3. Complete the payment process as directed on the website.
4. Once the payment is successful, you will receive a unique license key.
